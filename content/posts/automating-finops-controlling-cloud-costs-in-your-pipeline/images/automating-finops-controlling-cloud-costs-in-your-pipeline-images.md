# Image Prompts for "Automating FinOps: Controlling Cloud Costs in Your Pipeline"

## 1. Photorealistic / Cinematic
*   **Concept:** A macro shot of a digital coin or a holographic dollar sign floating above a circuit board or server rack. The lighting should be gold and cool blue, symbolizing value and technology.
*   **Prompt:** high quality, masterpiece, 8k resolution, sharp focus, photorealistic close-up of a glowing holographic golden dollar sign hovering over a complex computer circuit board, blue tech ambient lighting, depth of field, macro lens, highly detailed metallic textures, --ar 16:9
*   **Negative Prompt:** text, watermark, blurry, low quality, deformed, ugly, bad anatomy, paper money, people, messy.

## 2. 3D Abstract / Isometric
*   **Concept:** An isometric visualization of a cloud infrastructure (servers, databases) with a "shield" or "gate" made of golden light blocking excess usage. Or a balance scale weighing code blocks against gold coins.
*   **Prompt:** high quality, masterpiece, 8k resolution, sharp focus, 3D isometric render, a balanced scale weighing glowing code blocks on one side and gold coins on the other, set on a clean platform, soft studio lighting, pastel colors, clean rendering, Spline 3D style, --ar 16:9
*   **Negative Prompt:** text, watermark, blurry, low quality, deformed, ugly, jagged edges, noise, dark shadows, realistic people.

## 3. Minimalist Vector / Flat
*   **Concept:** A clean line-art style diagram showing a pipeline with a magnifying glass or a price tag icon attached to one of the steps.
*   **Prompt:** high quality, masterpiece, 8k resolution, sharp focus, minimalist vector art, flat design, a continuous line forming a pipeline flow with a price tag symbol integrated into the path, white lines on a dark blue background, simple geometric shapes, tech icon style, --ar 16:9
*   **Negative Prompt:** text, watermark, blurry, low quality, deformed, ugly, shading, gradients, 3d effects, realistic details, clutter.
